Biography

Exiszt is the musical-adjacent project of Gray Goliszek currently based out of North Carolina. He grew up as the roadie of punk bank Slappin’ Grama thanks to the euphonious involvement of their dad, which led to a lifetime of passion for live music. Some of their biggest influences derive from these early experiences. He feels that both artistically & personality-wise, he represents a mix between Donatello & Michaelangelo of the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles. Exiszt sets include a carefully curated selection of his favorite music showcased alongside original sounds. Styles tend to vary but generally one could expect to hear something that is almost, but not quite, entirely unlike Drum & Bass or Halftime.

Notable Apperances

With performances accross North & South America, Exiszt has shared the stage alongside: Truth, Monty, Spoonbill, Champagne Drip, The Widdler, Vinja, Kll Smth, Drrtywulvz, Duffrey, Zebbler Encanti Experience, Ace Aura, Pheel, Danny Grooves, Smigonaut, Bricksquash, Vide, INSPECT3R, Pathwey
